Meet The Team

Robin Gupta

Co-Founder and CEO

​Robin is a versatile Engineering leader with more than 15 years of experience in software delivery across startups, scale-ups and enterprises. Beyond work, he mentors at ADPList and Plato, contributes to open-source projects like Selenium, created TestZeus (the only open-source test automation framework for Salesforce), and has authored books and courses on the topic of Software testing and automation. He is also recognized as a speaker at international events such as Dreamforce (by Salesforce) and Selenium Conference. Robin is the Chief Executive Officer at TestArmy, and is fighting against the battle for bad quality software.

Shriyansh Agnihotri

Co-Founder and CTO

​Shriyansh is an accomplished technology leader with over 13 years of experience in distributed software development, scalable architecture design, and big data systems. As a co-founder and CTO, he excels in building products from the ground up, leading teams, and driving technical innovation. Shriyansh has successfully developed large-scale systems capable of handling petabytes of data, from proof of concept to full deployment. His expertise spans distributed systems, AI/Gen AI, and microservices,  from organizations such as Amagi and Nutanix. Beyond work, he is a true coffee aficionado and hardware geek.
